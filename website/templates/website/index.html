Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do we do?

    We build websites for small to medium-sized business. A modern website is the gateway that connects your business to the world.

  • How much is it going to cost?

    At the most basic level, we offer free websites. For additional features, we charge a once-off fee. On top of that, if you need help with hosting, we charge a small monthly fee.

  • How are the websites built?

    I use HTML templates available freely online, that work right out of the box, and I connect the template to a Django backend. This allows me to connect the template to a database, send email, and an admin panel, giving me more flexibility in building these websites.
